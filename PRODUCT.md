# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Dutch SMB owners and entrepreneurs looking for a reliable web development partner. They're practical, time-constrained, and skeptical of overpriced agencies. They want a site that works, looks good, and brings in leads — delivered without drama. They compare a few bureaus before choosing, and trust matters as much as portfolio.

## Product Purpose

BlueStar Development's marketing landing page. Purpose: convert visitors into leads (schedule a call / contact form). Success = a visitor feels confident enough in BlueStar's quality and reliability to reach out.

## Brand Personality

Scherp, snel, professioneel. Sharp execution, no fluff. The confidence of a senior developer who doesn't need to oversell. Approachable but not casual. Results-oriented.

## Anti-references

- Cheap template sites (Wix feel): stock photos, generic blue, no craft.
- Corporate IT companies: grey, cold, full of jargon — feels distant and transactional.

## Design Principles

1. **Practice what you preach.** A web dev agency's own site is the ultimate portfolio piece. Every pixel should signal quality.
2. **Confidence over decoration.** Let the work speak. Remove anything that decorates without adding clarity or trust.
3. **Speed as identity.** Fast load, snappy interactions, no layout shift. Performance is a brand value, not just a metric.
4. **Precision over abundance.** Tight spacing, clear hierarchy, nothing wasted. Restraint is the craft signal.
5. **Earn the scroll.** Each section earns attention before the next one asks for it.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Dutch-language primary, English toggle present. Reduced-motion handled throughout.
